Korean Beef Bowl

Description

This delicious Korean beef bowl is quick and easy to make with ground beef. It's seasoned with fresh ginger, garlic, soy, sesame seeds, and crushed red pepper, but these ingredients can easily be adjusted to suit your taste. Serve over warm rice or spiralized vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 5 cloves crushed garlic
  • 1 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • ½ cup reduced-sodium soy sause
  • ⅓ cup light brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 6 green onions, chopped
  • 4 cups hot cooked brown rice
  • 1 tbsp roasted sesame seeds

How to make

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Add beef and cook, stirring and crumbling into small pieces until browned
  3. Stir in garlic, ginger, and sesame oil and cook until fragrant
  4. Stir in soy sauce, brown sugar, and red pepper
  5. Cook ultil beef absorbs some sauce (about 7 minutes)
  6. Add ½ of chopped green onions
  7. Serve over rice; garnish with sesame seeds and remaining green onions
