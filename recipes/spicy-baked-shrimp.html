Spicy Baked Shrimp

Description

These delicious Spicy Baked Shrimp make for a super quick and easy snack for any occasion. Enjoy them alone or as part of a meal.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 pound uncooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • cooking spray

How to make

  1. Whisk olive oil, Cajun seasoning, lemon juice, parsley, honey, soy sauce, and cayenne pepper together in a large bowl
  2. Add the shrimp and toss evenly to coat, then cover the bowl with plastic wrap and marinate in the fridge for 1 hour
  3. Pre-heat oven to 450C Farenheit / 230C
  4. Spray a baking dish with cooking spray
  5. Transfer shrimp into the prepared baking dish and pour any remaining marinade over top
  6. Bake in the oven until the shrimp are bright pink on the outside and the meat is opaque (should take roughly 10 minutes)
  7. Take out and leve to cool slightly before eating
