Steak and Ale Pie

Description

In the mood for a good old English classic? Then this recipe has you covered. Here's everything you need to prepare and cook an amazing Steak and Ale Pie.

Ingredients

For the filling

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 100g smoked bacon lardons or smoked bacon, chopped
  • 1kg beef shin, cut into large chunks
  • 2 onions, roughly chopped
  • 3 carrots, peeled and cut into large chunks
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tbsp malt or red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp brown miso paste (optional)
  • 400ml sweet brown ale
  • 600ml beef stock made from 2 stock cubes
  • Few time sprigs, stalks and leaves separated
  • 2 bay leaves

For the pastry

  • 700g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 2 tbsp English mustard powder
  • 150g lard
  • 150g butter
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten

How to make

  1. Heat the oven to 160C / 140C fan assisted / fas 3/
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large casserole dish
  3. Gently fry the bacon for 5 minutes until lightly browned, then remove and set aside
  4. In the same pan, brown the beef well in batches for 5-8 mins, adding more oil if needed, then remove and set aside
  5. Add the onions and carrots to the pan and cook on a low heat for 5 minutes
  6. Stir in the flour, then add the tomato purée, vinegar, and miso (if using)
  7. Tip the beef and bacon, along with any juices, back into the pan and give a good stir
  8. Add the stock and ale, and nestle in the thyme stalks and bay leaves
  9. Season generously and bring to a simmer
  10. Cook for about 2 hours, or until the meat is nice and tender
  11. Leave everything to cool slightly, then strain off about half the braising liquid into a separate container
  12. Leave to cool completely
